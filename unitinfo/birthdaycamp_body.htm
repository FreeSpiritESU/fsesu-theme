FreeSpirit 10 Year Birthday Camp

When : 30 August - 01 September 2013

Where :  Botany Bay Activity Centre, Botany Bay, Trelleck Road, Tintern, Chepstow, NP16 6SN

Cost : £40 

A Birthday Camp to celebrate 10 years of FreeSpirit Explorers. 

*******UPDATE*********

Due to limited sign up for this event we have had to rethink it.

We will now be camping at Botany Bay Scout Campsite near Tintern. 

The dates remain the same 30/08/13 - 01/09/13 but arriving Friday evening instead of morning. 

The plan is to have lots of different activities followed by a Hog roast and party on the Saturday night. Sunday will be a Big Boy Breakfast in true FreeSpirit Style followed by more activities.

The cost will be reduced to £40 for the full weekend. This will cover camping fee, activities and full catering. 

We want to see as many FreeSpirits as possible, so if you can only make part of the weekend you are still more than welcome. Whether it's for a day, an evening or just an hour, please contact us so we can arrange what cost (if any) will be involved.

If you have already sent in your form and paid the original £40 we will consider you coming and fully paid unless you tell us otherwise.
